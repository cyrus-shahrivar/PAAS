Positivity As A Service (PAAS)

A Positive, RESTful API

Introduction

There are many "$#|+... as a Service" offers in the tech industry and on the web right now, but what we need more than ever is what I call Positivity as a Service, or for short PAAS.

Documentation

On the left-hand side, that's what you type after www.paas.com to get a new page with a rendered bit of positivity based on what is on the right-hand side.

Example: www.pass.com/gm/Cyrus/Courtney produces "Good Morning, Cyrus! -Courtney"

To receive JSON formatted data insted of a custom bit of rendered positiviy, just type www.paas.com/api then the rest of the URL you want below.

/gm/:to/:from = "Good Morning, :to! -:from"

/gd/:to/:from = "Good Day, :to! -:from"

/ge/:to/:from = "Good Evening, :to! -:from"

/gn/:to/:from = "Good Night, :to! -:from"
